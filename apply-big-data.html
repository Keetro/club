WHO WE ARE

Keetro.club is a non-profit marketing club that provides career and training opportunities for ambitious sales development reps. Our mission is to promote the spread of professional communities and help everyone share their expertise. Everything we do is aimed at making sure talented and curious people everywhere, from entrepreneurs to yoga teachers, find it easy and convenient to reach their peers for meaningful conversations. We are looking for curiously brilliant individuals to join our Marketing club and help us reinvent how events are organized around the world.

This is an opportunity to work on something brand new, and unique, embracing AI, Big Data and Technology in your daily workflow. If you are passionate about marketing, offline events – let’s have a chat. This is a perfect role for any hard-working and ambitious person.

WHAT WE'VE GOT GOING ON

Keetro is seeking currently employed (full-time or part-time) Sales Development Representatives (SDRs) who aspire to a career in marketing. You have great communication and organization skills. Failure doesn’t deter you, you are always prepared and professional. You want to be the best.

In this part-time/contract role, you’ll manage parts of sales development process, but will also constantly test your limits performing new tasks in marketing. This is a part-time remote contract position with a variable compensation for meeting target objectives that are critical to the overall success of our keetro.org non-profit community.
